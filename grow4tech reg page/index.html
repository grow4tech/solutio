Solutio IoT Hackathon

Welcome to Solutio - The IoT Hackathon

Welcome to Solutio, a hackathon focused on IoT innovations! This event is an opportunity for students from BCA, MCA, and Engineering backgrounds to showcase their skills and creativity. Join us in solving real-world problems with cutting-edge IoT technology.

Eligibility

  • Open to students from BCA, MCA, and Engineering.
  • Teams of 1 to 5 members are allowed.
  • Participants must be able to travel to the finale location.

Registration Process

Register online starting January 8th at our registration portal.

Competition Rounds

  1. Round 1: Online test on Data Structures and Algorithms (3 hours).
  2. Round 2: Online test on System Design (3 hours).
  3. Round 3: Simulation problem related to Embedded Systems (5 hours).

The Finale

The 3-day finale includes:

  1. Day 1: Inauguration and problem statement presentation. Planning process begins.
  2. Day 2: Development and integration.
  3. Day 3: Project presentation and guest talks.

Registration Fee: Rs. 499
