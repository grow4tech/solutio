About the Hackathon

Solutio is an exciting opportunity for students from BCA, MCA, and Engineering backgrounds to showcase their skills in the rapidly evolving field of technology. Our hackathon invites bright minds to solve challenging problems and innovate for the future.

Registration is now open: Register Online

Eligibility Criteria

  • Open to students pursuing BCA, MCA, and Engineering.
  • Team participation ranging from solo individuals up to groups of three.
  • Ability to travel to the finale venue is required.

The Process

Registration Begins: 9th January. Sign up through our online portal.

Preliminary Rounds: Engage in three online qualifying rounds.

The Grand Finale: A three-day onsite event filled with intense development, innovation, and presentations.

Round Details

  • First Round: A 3-hour online test focusing on Data Structures and Algorithms.
  • Second Round: Another 3-hour online assessment based on System Design.
  • Third Round: A 5-hour challenge to solve a simulation problem related to Embedded Systems.

The Finale

  1. Day 1: Inauguration and unveiling of the problem statement, followed by the planning phase.
  2. Day 2: Development and integration.
  3. Day 3: Final presentations and guest speakers.

Rewards and Recognition

  • Certificates for all participants, with special recognition for those advancing through rounds.
  • The winning team or individual receives Rs. 2,00,000, an opportunity to work on a major project, and competitive market salary.
  • Top five teams may receive lucrative recruitment offers.

Accommodation

Accommodations provided for participants attending the finale.

Registration Fee: Rs. 500 per participant.
